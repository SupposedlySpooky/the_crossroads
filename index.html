With Whom am I Speaking?

I am an artist, game developer, musician, and whatever I need to be. Many people know me under the name Supposedly Spooky, under which I made my most sucessful game to date, They Grew Lungs and Drowned. I was born and raised in Nebraska, far from the water, and am currently figuring myself out in college. I find things like this to waste my time and find enjoyment in it all. I hope you enjoy it too!

What is Happening?

I work on a very loose doing-it-as-i-please schedule when it comes to things I create of my own time and money. After making games for four(4(wow)) years now, I can see a loose pattern in where I'll release one game per year, and scrap many more than that. This is just a pattern (which you can observe here), and is subject to deviance. I release music usually whenever I release a game, and I post art uh. Whenever I feel like it.

Reaching You

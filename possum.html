Shrine Dedicated to:

Possum



I'm the first to admit my horror movie catalog isn't what it should be. Limited to a handful stories of terror, Possum is still the standout tale that kept me awake for a good hour-and-a-half.
I think the true beauty of this film shines through its socially uncomfortable themes, framing, and of course, the masterfully crafted 'Possum'.
The film partially falls through near the end, when the plot is revealed in its full awfulness, but it's still amazing how it caries its tone straight to the end.
